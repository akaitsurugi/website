In the Bible, people received new names when significant changes occurred in the their lives.

But why would a website need a new name?

===

> Think not that I am come to send peace on earth: I came not to send peace, but a sword.  
<span class="reference">- Matthew 10:34</span>

The old name for this website, Christian Liberty Institute, was chosen quickly. I wanted to get a website started to see what I could do with web technologies that I had never used before. It described the purpose of the website, but as time went on I realized that it wasn't a good name and that it was too long. Account handles on other websites would create issues, since it was longer than fifteen characters. Some references to Christian Liberty Institute may linger for a while (for example, with our Courses), but these will eventually be updated.

## The New Name

![Akai Tsurugi in Japanese calligraphy](image://akai_calligraphy_vertical.png?lightbox&resize=140,280){.right}

Akai Tsurugi is the transliteration (Latin characters used to represent another language) of the Japanese words 赤い (_akai_, red) and 剣 (_tsurugi_, sword).

### Why Japanese?

Although this website is only available in English now, Japanese will be the first non-English language we will support with Japanese resources in the future.

### Why a sword?

> For the word of God _is_ quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and _is_ a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart.  
<span class="reference">- Hebrews 4:12</span>

The Bible is referred to as a sword in Scripture. The focus of this website is to provide resources to aid in the study of the Bible. And swords are cool. When a new logo is made for the website, it will feature a sword.

### Why a red sword?

As I learned from the crayon box song: red is for the color of the blood that He shed.

Ultimately, the Bible is about Christ and His blood. The doctrine of the blood atonement has been assaulted by people who claim to be Christians, saying that the atonement has nothing to do with the blood or that blood is just a "symbol" of His death. Don't let those liberals fool you. What Christ accomplished through His sinless life, shed blood, vicarious death, burial, and resurrection (also referred to as the work of Christ) is the core of the Bible and the foundation of eternal salvation. No wonder it is under attack by Satan and his devils.
