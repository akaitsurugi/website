This book challenges the widely-held influence of Cyrus Ingerson Scofield's Bible notes, which the author believes were based on a significant deception. The author, with 65 years of ordained ministry and biblical study, aims to expose the errors and presumptions in the Scofield Reference Bible, which have shaped the direction of churches, especially in America.

===

Drawing from extensive research, including previously unpublished sources, this book provides a critical examination of Scofield's life, credentials, and teachings. Despite being one of the most influential figures in Evangelical history, Scofield's life and work have been largely unexamined, with only a handful of sources available until recent years. This book seeks to fill that gap, offering an investigation on the man and his legacy.
