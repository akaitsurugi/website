A Japanese translation of the New Testament based on the Textus Receptus.

===

> 初めに**言葉**あり、**言葉**は**神**と共にあり、**言葉**は**神**であった。  
<span class="reference">ヨハネ　1.1</span>

This can be used with SWORD project [Bible study software](http://crosswire.org/applications/?target=_blank&rel=noopener&rel=noreferrer) such as [Eloquent](http://eloquent-bible-study.eu/Eloquent_Bible_Study/Eloquent_Bible_Study.html?target=_blank&rel=noopener&rel=noreferrer) (MacSword) and [Xiphos](https://xiphos.org/?target=_blank&rel=noopener&rel=noreferrer) by downloading from the CrossWire module repository, or by manually installing the [SWORD module](sword/japlifeline-1.3-sword.zip). The source used to create that and these pages can be found on the [Git repository](https://github.com/akaitsurugi/japlifeline/?target=_blank&rel=noopener&rel=noreferrer).

The Lifeline Japanese New Testament was published by [Gospel to Japan](http://www.gospeltojapan.org/?target=_blank&rel=noopener&rel=noreferrer) and the descriptions below are from them.

This translation has been based on the same Greek texts used in the New Testament of the King James Bible, and for those who have been frustrated with the deletions, omissions, and weaker translation of the current Japanese translations, this will be a blessing to have. Many years of labor have gone into its research, and it is being provided at only the cost of shipping.
