If you are new here, please visit the [About](/about) page and the [Blog](/blog) to learn about Akai Tsurugi and our beliefs.

Our goal is to help a Christian, whether a baby or mature believer, to attain an education superior to what seminaries or Bible colleges are offering—but for free.

The majority of our content can be found under the [Library](/library): a collection of free, Biblically-based educational resources. You will never be required to create an account to access the files shared here. Far too often "Christian" organizations charge a price for their data, which we find to contradict what the Scriptures teach.

> For the love of money is the root of all evil: which while some coveted after, they have erred from the faith, and pierced themselves through with many sorrows.  
<span class="reference">- I Timothy 6:10</span>
