Michael Gowens was ordained to the ministry in Texas in 1981. He currently pastors Bethel Primitive Baptist Church in Calabash, North Carolina. He has previously served as church pastor in Smithville, Georgia and Lexington, Kentucky. Gowens has authored over a dozen books, conducted a radio ministry, and traveled moderately among churches in America, the Philippines, and Africa.
